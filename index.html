Nutrition and Diets

Keeping your body fit and well-nourished with proper nutrition and following suitable diets is of utmost importance during pregnancy. At Karpadharani, you’ll be introduced to all types of nutrition elements and preferable diets for pregnancy, prenatal, post-natal, and baby care. Saying “YES” to healthy nutrition plans, and working together with our diet instructors and advisors will ensure you a peaceful and hassle-free experience at our centre. Our experts will guide you to the end with all the proper detailing and advising required for proper nutrition and preferable diets.

FAQ

  • What are Pre-pregnancy Care Classes?

    If you’re thinking about getting married or going to be pregnant soon, or sometime in the future, it’s important to start making healthy choices now. Our goal is to provide information before the pregnancy phase in order to improve a woman’s health, help her plan for pregnancy, help reduce risks to her future baby and accompany her sail comfortably through the first three months of pregnancy. Our classes will help enlighten both men and women to overcome hurdles and live successfully through the pregnancy phase.

  • What are the benefits of attending these classes?

    Babies begin to develop even before women know that they are pregnant. You may find out you that are pregnant when your baby is already 5-8 weeks old. This means that the time to prepare for your baby’s health starts way before you even get pregnant. The healthier you and your partner are at the time of conception, the better chance your baby will have to be healthy too! During our informative classes, you will learn about the factors that contribute to or hinder your overall pregnancy health and that of your body, as well as help you put any risk factors into perspective and how to deal with them.

  • How is Pre-Pregnancy Class different from Pregnancy Care Class?

    Pregnancy Care Classes can help you after the 3rd month of pregnancy but pre-pregnancy classes help you to equip yourself before you plan for a pregnancy and gear you up to face early pregnancy smoothly till the time you can attend a pregnancy care classes.

  • Who are eligible for Pre-pregnancy Care Classes?

    Any woman of childbearing age before her marriage or before planning for pregnancy or in between pregnancies is eligible to attend these classes.

Who are we

A secured pregnancy awareness centre in Coimbatore, Karpadharani is the hub for providing an in-depth understanding of prenatal, post-natal and parenting topics. Our expert doctors hold classes imparting professional and expert advice to the people along with a professional qualified faculty team.
Unlike other prenatal classes, we focus more on traditional and scientifically proven techniques, which shall not only help carrying out normal deliveries but also help in aiding and boosting relationships between mothers and their new-borns.
One of our most unique programs is the Antenatal program which deals in the preparation of couples for birth and helps them with the essential care required after-birth. This program eventually takes care of all aspects of birth and thereby making the process easier and helpful in the long term.
We also hold expertise in specialized couple plans which will help in bringing out unique bonding experiences and a feeling of togetherness.
