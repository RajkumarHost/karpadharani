Top notch
pregnancy classes

Karpadharani centre help the expecting mothers to be stable, clear and confident. She gets information about what changes she is undergoing, common pregnancy discomforts and comforting measures.

Our classes allow women not only to share their concerns and feelings, but also to get informations about what they will go through during labour and birth. Through interactive adult learning methods, we provide parents-to-be with all prenatal information and help them understand how to cope with the baby once it is born and deal with all postnatal changes and issues.
